# Agri-Logix: Executive Pitch Brief

**The Problem:** 
Zimbabwe’s Pfumvudza input subsidy programme distributes millions of dollars in seed annually, but manual paper-based tracking leads to massive leakage, delayed payments to seed houses (up to 12 months), and counterfeit seeds entering the supply chain.

**The Solution:** 
Agri-Logix SeedTracker is a blockchain-anchored, mobile-first supply chain verification platform. It assigns unique QR codes to certified seed bags at the manufacturer, tracking them all the way to the farmer’s hands. 

**How It Works:**
1. **Seed Houses** print and attach QR codes to seed bags.
2. **Distribution** is tracked via GPS and batch scanning.
3. **Extension Officers** register farmers digitally.
4. **Farmers** verify and redeem their seeds via USSD (*123#) on any basic mobile phone—no internet required.

**Key Value Propositions:**
- **For Government:** Treasury-ready reconciliation reports, eliminating ghost farmers and cutting reconciliation time from 6 months to 1 week.
- **For Seed Houses:** Instant proof-of-delivery enabling 30-day payment cycles.
- **For Farmers:** Guaranteed authentic seed and SMS confirmation of their allocations.

**Job Creation & Economic Impact:**
- **Employment:** Creates thousands of local tech-enabled jobs (Data Clerks, Verification Agents, IT Support, Logistics).
- **Savings:** Saves the government millions by eliminating up to 20% input diversion.
- **Food Security:** Boosts national yields by ensuring high-yield, authentic seeds reach the right soil.

**Traction & Readiness:**
- **Pilot Ready:** 4 web dashboards (Government, Seed House, Extension Officer, Farmer), a Flutter mobile app, and a USSD interface are fully built and live.
- **Next Step:** District-level pilot with 1 seed house and 5,000 farmers.
