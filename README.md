# AGRI-LOGIX SeedTracker

Blockchain-verified seed tracking for Zimbabwe's Pfumvudza programme. QR-coded bags, USSD redemption on any phone, SHA-256 audit trail, GPS verification, Treasury-ready reports. Pilot-ready. Works offline. No smartphone required.

## Impact and Job Creation
Deploying Agri-Logix at a national scale will not only secure the agricultural supply chain but also stimulate significant local employment:
- **Verification Agents & Data Clerks:** Thousands of youths can be employed at distribution points to assist elderly farmers with USSD redemption and QR scanning.
- **Digital Extension Officers:** Upskilling Agritex officers with mobile technology, creating specialized tech-agri roles.
- **IT Support & Infrastructure:** Local developers, system administrators, and network technicians required to maintain the national database and troubleshoot USSD/Firebase integrations.
- **Logistics Coordinators:** Supply chain managers required at the district level to monitor real-time dashboards and re-route seed deliveries based on live data.
- **Counterfeit Investigators:** Specialized roles utilizing the blockchain audit trail to track down and prosecute black market seed syndicates.

By eliminating up to 20% of input diversion, Agri-Logix effectively pays for these new roles through direct savings to the Treasury.
